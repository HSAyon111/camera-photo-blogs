pic 1: Lighting a fire in Winter

In January 2021, I went to my village home and stayed there for a few days. That was the time of winter season. In the evening of day 2, me and my freinds gatthered some woods. Then we set them on fire for enlighting the place. Also we warmed our hands and bodies by the fire flames. The outside weather was cold so the burning woods was helpful to increase the temparatures. That time I clicked this photo with my camera. The day was quite enjoyable.
